Ensemble Documentation

FuzzyStock AI utilizes a hybrid weighting system powered by **Yahoo Finance** price streams. The engine calculates technical convergence across multiple indicators to provide alignment-based signals.

The Intelligence Engine

The ensemble model processes five primary technical factors. When indicator synergy is detected, the confidence score increases, reflecting a high-conviction environment. The system is designed for high-privacy, processing all data on-device through the unified pipeline.

Symbols

Use Yahoo’s ticker format: US stocks (AAPL), indices (^GSPC, ^NSEI), or exchange suffixes (e.g. RELIANCE.NS for NSE).

Yahoo data is unofficial and may change; this is suitable for research and educational purposes.

Risk Management

Confidence scores are relative to signal alignment. Users are encouraged to cross-reference multiple timeframes for maximum clarity.
